--- title: What we do, in brief ---

Brain imaging needs behavior

We design and validate novel tasks for onsite and web-based assessment of cognitive abilities and brain functioning, in combination with neuroimaging and neurosurgical procedures.

Virtual worlds for a new neuropsychology

We develop interactive, immersive experiences for a new generation of neuropsychological tests, and for studying the brain in more ecological settings.

A passion for brain maps

We study individual brains in detail to identify specific cortical regions, their functions, and their connections.

Brain connections at work

We leverage advanced analytics to evaluate models of how brain regions communicate and exert influence on each other to support mental computations.
